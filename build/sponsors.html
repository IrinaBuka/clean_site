THANK YOU TO OUR SPONSORS

Special companies make the great things happen at the Downtown Detroit Parks and in return for their generous support of this exceptional urban park and its outstanding community programs, these beautiful public spaces honor them as official partners. This special designation enables them to reach out with their corporate messages via high-profile and exciting ways throughout metro Detroit, activate their brand programs on-site in one of America's top public spaces and directly engage thousands of people through a number of world-class events held in the heart of the city. The Downtown Detroit Parks create wonderful relationships with its partners, ensuring that these spaces receive the recognition they deserve. Official partnership opportunities are available for a variety of involvement levels. If interested, please contact us to discuss the details of sponsorship participation.
